cwd

cwd — short for current working directory — is where everything I make ends up. Right now that's helio, a weather app I want to feel calmer than the rest, with a notes engine (mossy), a plain-text finance CLI (ledger), and a couple of experiments — drift, postbox — in various states of done.

Most start as a folder and a vague idea. A few grow into something real; the rest just sit here, which is sort of the point — they all land in the same directory either way. I keep notes on the mess in field-notes.

cwd is the personal labs of @kierandrewett. Also on GitHub, or say hello.
